少林过时了么？

这是一个我没有答案的问题，不过，这确实是一个困扰我很久的问题。

金庸的大部分小说里都有少林派，而且大部分小说里都说少林是第一大派。金庸说少林有七十二绝

技，有易经筋，有很多很厉害的武功。

可是，那些少林和尚很少有武功天下第一的，除了那个身份存疑的扫地僧外。

少林有专门研究本派武功的达摩堂，海域专门研究其他派武功的般若堂，还有专门打架的罗汉堂，还

有收藏各种典籍的藏经阁。

这么丰满的结构，为什么研究不出“弹指神通”？研究不出“黯然销魂掌”？研究不出“蛤蟆功”？研究不出

“龙象般若功”？

这些武功，都是一些很小的机构，甚至是一些个人研究出来的。比如黄药师的弹指神通，杨过的黯然

销魂掌。为什么这些这么高级的武功，都是出自那些看起来毫无研究能力的小机构和个人呢？

如果说，少林也研究出来了相当的武功，只是不是那些也算，问题是，少林好像什么都没研究出来，

几百年过来了，还是那些七十二绝技，没增没减。

但几百年了，桃花岛花开花谢，杨少侠钻进古墓，少林却仿佛会一直这么下去：没有新的武功，却一

直这么长存。

为什么呢？

9 AUGUST

Research Laboratory: Obsolete?

Technologies crisscross industries and travel incredibly fast.

What accounts for the decline in the number of major corporate re-search labs?

The company-owned research laboratory was one of the nineteenth century’s most successful

inventions.

Now many research directors, as well as high-tech industrialists, tend to believe that such labs are

becoming obsolete.

Why? Technologies crisscross industries and travel incredibly fast, making few of them unique

anymore.

And increasingly, the knowledge needed in a given industry comes out of some totally different

technology with which, very often, the people in the industry are quite unfamiliar.

As a result the big research labs of the past are becoming obsolete.

The research laboratory of the big telephone companies, the famous Bell Laboratories of the U.S.,

was for many decades the source of all major innovations in the telephone industry.

But no one in that industry worked on fiberglass cables or had ever heard of them.

They were developed by a glass company, Corning.

Yet they have revolutionized communications worldwide.

Scan the environment for a technology developed in another industry that can help you now.
