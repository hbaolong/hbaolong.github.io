contravene vs contradict

超清楚对比表（2025日常+法律英语）

项目 contravene contradict
核心意思 违反、违背（规则、法律、条约） 反驳、否认、与……相矛盾（陈述、事实）
对象 法律、规定、协议、义务 某人的话、证据、说法、逻辑
法律性 强（常构成违法） 无（只是逻辑/事实冲突）
语气强度 正式、严肃（法庭、合同常用） 中性到正式（日常争论也用）
常见搭配 contravene a law / treaty / article / regulation contradict a statement / witness / oneself
后果 可能被罚款、起诉 只是“显得撒谎”或“逻辑错误”

超好记的例句对比

句子 用哪个？为什么？
His actions contravene Article 5 of the treaty. 违反条约 → contravene
Her testimony contradicts what she said yesterday. 证词前后矛盾 → contradict
Smoking here contravenes company policy. 违反公司规定 → contravene
The two reports contradict each other. 两份报告互相打架 → contradict
You can’t contravene international law and expect no sanctions. 违反国际法 → contravene
He contradicted himself three times in five minutes. 自相矛盾 → contradict

相关词家族（一网打尽）

词根方向 contravene 家族（违反） contradict 家族（反驳）
动词 contravene contradict
名词 contravention contradiction
形容词 — contradictory
常用短语 in contravention of the rules a flat / direct contradiction
近义词 violate, breach, infringe deny, rebut, gainsay, belie
法律专用 contravene a statute / regulation self-contradictory evidence

一句话彻底记住

  • contravene = 踩红线、违法、违约（警察叔叔要管）
  • contradict = 说的话打架（别人翻白眼就行）

Here are some natural sentences that use both contravene and contradict together (2025 real-world style):

  1. His public statement contradicts everything he told the committee in private and contravenes the non-disclosure agreement he signed.

  2. The minister’s new policy not only contradicts her earlier promises but also contravenes Article 14 of the constitution.

  3. The company’s claim that “no data was lost” contradicts the forensic report and contravenes GDPR disclosure rules.

  4. If you sign this clause, you cannot later contradict it in court without contravening the entire contract.

  5. The witness contradicted himself three times and, in doing so, contravened his oath to tell the whole truth.

  6. The proposed law contradicts the government’s own manifesto and would contravene international human-rights obligations.
