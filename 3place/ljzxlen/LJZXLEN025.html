4.5 It now appears possible to specify the most general propositional form: that is, to give a description of the propositions of any sign-language, so that every possible sense can be expressed by a symbol that fits the description, and that every symbol that fits the description can express a sense, if the meanings of the names are chosen accordingly.

    • 现在我们就有可能明确指出一般命题的结构了：对于任何记号语言的命题来说，我们可以给出一个描述，在这个描述中，每一个可能的 意义都可以用适合描述的符号来标识，只好我们把名字和意义做精心的选择，那么每一个符合描述的符号就可以表达一个意义了。

    It is clear that in the description of the most general propositional form only what is essential to it may be described—since otherwise it would not be the most general.

    • 对于最常见的命题的结构的描述来说，最重要的就是它是可描述的，如果不是，那么，他就不是‘常见的命题’了。

      That there is a general propositional form is demonstrated by this: that there cannot be a proposition whose form could not have been foreseen (i.e. constructed). The general form of a proposition is: such and such is the case.

    • 一个通常的命题可以这样表达：命题不存在不可预知的结构。命题的通常结构应该是这样的，或者那样的。

  • foresee,英 [fɔːˈsiː] 美 [fɔːrˈsiː] v. 预见，预知

      4.51 Suppose that I am given all elementary propositions; then the question arises as to what propositions I can form from them. And the answer is all propositions and this is how they are limited.

    • 假如我得到了所有的基础命题的列表，那么，我的问题就变成了：我可以用这些基础命题组成哪些命题？答案肯定是：所有的命题。这就是对明天的约束。

      4.52 Propositions are everything that follows from the totality of all elementary propositions (and also, of course, from its being the totality of all). (So, in a certain sense, one could say that all propositions are generalizations of elementary propositions.)

    • 命题就是所有基础命题的自然产物，当然，它自己也就是一切。（从一定意义上，可以说所有的命题都是由基础命题派生的）。

      4.53 The general propositional form is a variable.
    • 一般命题形式，就是一个变量。
