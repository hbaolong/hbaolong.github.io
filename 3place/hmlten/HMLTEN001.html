INTRODUCTION

About 500 years ago, Hamlet’s father, the king of Denmark, was murdered by his own brother, Claudius. Then Claudius quickly married Hamlet’s mother, Gertrude. As the play opens, Hamlet’s father’s ghost appears and tells his son who murdered him. He urges Hamlet to seek revenge. As the play unfolds, Hamlet tries to convince himself that he should murder Claudius.

本章的重点单词学习

This is Shakespeare’s most famous play, known for the anguished character of Hamlet.
