旧约 – 传道书(Ecclesiastes) – 第 7 章 ( 本篇共有 12 章 )

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:1 名誉强如美好的膏油；人死的日子胜过人生的日子。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:1 A good name is better than precious ointment; and the day of death than the day of one’s birth.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:1 A good name is better than fine perfume, and the day of death better than the day of birth.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:1 A good name is better than a good ointment, And the day of one’s death is better than the day of one’s birth.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:2 往遭丧的家去，强如往宴乐的家去，因为死是众人的结局，活人也必将这事放在心上。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:2 It is better to go to the house of mourning, than to go to the house of feasting: for that is the end of all men; and the living will lay it to his heart.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:2 It is better to go to a house of mourning than to go to a house of feasting, for death is the destiny of every man; the living should take this to heart.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:2 It is better to go to a house of mourning Than to go to a house of feasting, Because that is the end of every man, And the living takes it to heart.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:3 忧愁强如喜笑，因为面带愁容，终必使心喜乐。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:3 Sorrow is better than laughter: for by the sadness of the countenance the heart is made better.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:3 Sorrow is better than laughter, because a sad face is good for the heart.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:3 Sorrow is better than laughter, For when a face is sad a heart may be happy.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:4 智慧人的心，在遭丧之家；愚昧人的心在快乐之家。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:4 The heart of the wise is in the house of mourning; but the heart of fools is in the house of mirth.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:4 The heart of the wise is in the house of mourning, but the heart of fools is in the house of pleasure.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:4 The mind of the wise is in the house of mourning, While the mind of fools is in the house of pleasure.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:5 听智慧人的责备，强如听愚昧人的歌唱。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:5 It is better to hear the rebuke of the wise, than for a man to hear the song of fools.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:5 It is better to heed a wise man’s rebuke than to listen to the song of fools.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:5 It is better to listen to the rebuke of a wise man Than for one to listen to the song of fools.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:6 愚昧人的笑声，好像锅下烧荆棘的爆声，这也是虚空。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:6 For as the crackling of thorns under a pot, so is the laughter of the fool: this also is vanity.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:6 Like the crackling of thorns under the pot, so is the laughter of fools. This too is meaningless.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:6 For as the crackling of thorn bushes under a pot, So is the laughter of the fool; And this too is futility.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:7 勒索使智慧人变为愚妄，贿赂能败坏人的慧心。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:7 Surely oppression maketh a wise man mad; and a gift destroyeth the heart.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:7 Extortion turns a wise man into a fool, and a bribe corrupts the heart.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:7 For oppression makes a wise man mad, And a bribe corrupts the heart.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:8 事情的终局强如事情的起头；存心忍耐的，胜过居心骄傲的。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:8 Better is the end of a thing than the beginning thereof: and the patient in spirit is better than the proud in spirit.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:8 The end of a matter is better than its beginning, and patience is better than pride.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:8 The end of a matter is better than its beginning; Patience of spirit is better than haughtiness of spirit.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:9 你不要心里急躁恼怒，因为恼怒存在愚昧人的怀中。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:9 Be not hasty in thy spirit to be angry: for anger resteth in the bosom of fools.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:9 Do not be quickly provoked in your spirit, for anger resides in the lap of fools.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:9 Do not be eager in your heart to be angry, For anger resides in the bosom of fools.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:10 不要说，先前的日子强过如今的日子，是什么缘故呢？你这样问，不是出于智慧。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:10 Say not thou, What is the cause that the former days were better than these? for thou dost not inquire wisely concerning this.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:10 Do not say, “Why were the old days better than these?“ For it is not wise to ask such questions.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:10 Do not say, “Why is it that the former days were better than these?“ For it is not from wisdom that you ask about this.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:11 智慧和产业并好，而且见天日的人，得智慧更为有益。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:11 Wisdom is good with an inheritance: and by it there is profit to them that see the sun.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:11 Wisdom, like an inheritance, is a good thing and benefits those who see the sun.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:11 Wisdom along with an inheritance is good And an advantage to those who see the sun.

  • 〔CUV〕 传 7:12 因为智慧护庇人，好像银钱护庇人一样。惟独智慧能保全智慧人的生命。这就是知识的益处。

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:12 For wisdom is a defense, and money is a defense: but the excellency of knowledge is, that wisdom giveth life to them that have it.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:12 Wisdom is a shelter as money is a shelter, but the advantage of knowledge is this: that wisdom preserves the life of its possessor.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:12 For wisdom is protection just as money is protection, But the advantage of knowledge is that wisdom preserves the lives of its possessors.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:13 Consider what God has done: Who can straighten what he has made crooked?

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:13 Consider the work of God, For who is able to straighten what He has bent?

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:14 In the day of prosperity be joyful, but in the day of adversity consider: God also hath set the one over against the other, to the end that man should find nothing after him.

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:14 When times are good, be happy; but when times are bad, consider: God has made the one as well as the other. Therefore, a man cannot discover anything about his future.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:14 In the day of prosperity be happy, But in the day of adversity consider– God has made the one as well as the other So that man will not discover anything that will be after him.

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:15 All things have I seen in the days of my vanity: there is a just man that perisheth in his righteousness, and there is a wicked man that prolongeth his life in his wickedness.

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:15 I have seen everything during my lifetime of futility; there is a righteous man who perishes in his righteousness and there is a wicked man who prolongs his life in his wickedness.

  • 〔KJV〕 Ecc 7:17 Be not over much wicked, neither be thou foolish: why shouldest thou die before thy time?

  • 〔NIV〕 Ecc 7:17 Do not be overwicked, and do not be a fool– why die before your time?

  • 〔NASB〕 Ecc 7:17 Do not be excessively wicked and do not be a fool. Why should you die before your time?
