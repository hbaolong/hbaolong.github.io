教主是谁的教主？

张无忌是明教教主，武功天下第一。明教的军队打下天下后，却是朱元璋做了皇帝，张无忌据说找了

个地方绣花去了。为什么作为教主，最后却靠边站了？

史火龙是丐帮帮主，陈友谅找了一个人假冒他，龙头，掌钵长老居然都没有看出来，还被他耍的团团

转，史火龙死了大家都不知道，丐帮究竟姓陈？还是姓史？

这些看起来很奇怪的事情，其实都是自然的一个结果，都是因为这些位高权重的人的一个错误判断。

张无忌知道自己是明教的教主，却不知道明教不但有左右使，有四法王，有五散人，还有五行旗的几

十万人；张无忌虽然做了教主，其实只是做了光明顶的教主，而没有作到明教真正的教主。明教的几

十万教众，他们对张无忌就不大感冒，他们认的是哪些一直带着他们打仗的朱元璋、常遇春、甚至陈

友谅。

张无忌凭借武功和人脉成为了明教教主，却把注意力过多集中到了高层，也就是光明顶上闹来闹去的

那些人，什么青翼蝠王，什么光明左使，甚至放下激战中的明教，带着庞大的管理层去满满大海去迎

接金毛狮王。

愣是把一个大教的教主当成了光明顶的办公室主任。

史火龙更过分，明明是几十万丐帮的帮主，硬是做成了2-3个丐帮高层的经理人。而且，这些高层还都

不大认识他。

这是一个很奇怪的事情，当张无忌做教主时，当史火龙做帮主时，他们如何看自己的教和帮呢？

光明左右使，四法王等，当然是自己的教众，是自己需要考虑的，几十万教众也是自己要考虑的；四

大长老当然是自己要考虑的，几十万丐帮的乞丐也是自己的属下。

再说说萧峰，需要和高层商量时，绝对不含糊，没事时，就会去和低袋的弟子一起喝酒，因为他知

道，自己的帮众，不止有哪些八袋九袋长老，还有那些一袋二袋弟子。

Defining Business Purpose and Mission:

The Customer

Who is the customer?

“Who is the customer?” is the first and the crucial question in defining business purpose and

business mission.

crucial：

至关重要的；关键性的

(to/for sth) | ~ (that…) extremely important, because it will affect other things

It is not an easy, let alone an obvious question.

let alone：更不必说；听任；不打扰

How it is being answered determines, in large measure, how the business defines itself.

The consumer—that is, the ultimate user of a product or a service—is always a customer.

ultimate：

最后的；最终的；终极的

happening at the end of a long process

Most businesses have at least two customers.

Both have to buy if there is to be a sale.

The manufacturers of branded consumer goods always have two customers at the very least: the

housewife and the grocer.

It does not do much good to have the housewife eager to buy if the grocer does not stock the

brand.

Conversely, it does not do much good to have the grocer display merchandise advantageously

and give it shelf space if the housewife does not buy.

merchandise：

商品；货品

( formal ) goods that are bought or sold; goods that are for sale in a shop/store

To satisfy only one of these customers without satisfying the other means that there is no

performance.

ACTION POINT:

Take one product or service that you are responsible for and determine how many kinds of

customers you have for it.

Then figure out if you are satisfying all of your different kinds of customers, or if you are ignoring

some category(ies) of customers.

The Customer

Who is the customer?

“Who is the customer?” is the first and the crucial question in defining

business purpose and business mission. It is not an easy, let alone an obvious

question. How it is being answered determines, in large measure, how the

business defines itself. The consumer—that is, the ultimate user of

a product or a service—is always a customer.

Most businesses have at least two customers. Both have to buy if there

is to be a sale. The manufacturers of branded consumer goods always

have two customers at the very least: the housewife and the grocer. It

does not do much good to have the housewife eager to buy if the grocer

does not stock the brand. Conversely, it does not do much good to have

the grocer display merchandise advantageously and give it shelf space if

the housewife does not buy. To satisfy only one of these customers

without satisfying the other means that there is no performance.

ACTION POINT:

Take one product or service that you are responsible for

and determine how many kinds of customers you have for it. Then figure

out if you are satisfying all of your different kinds of customers, or if you

are ignoring some category(ies) of customers.
