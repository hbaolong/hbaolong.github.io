少林为什么要有俗家弟子

少林寺是一个宗教机构，也是一个武学机构。少林有七十二绝技，有专门研究高级武功的达摩堂，也

有研究外派武功的般若堂，还有教授少林弟子的罗汉堂，还有收藏武学书籍的藏经阁。

少林寺这座寺庙里有大量的和尚，有大量的武功，这些大量的和尚在反复练习着大量的武功，所以，

少林寺里有很多高手。

少林寺里有两大武学研究机构，也就是达摩院和般若堂。这两个机构从少林寺一开始就有了。上千年

来，这两个机构也研究出了很多武功，这些武功不断强化着少林大和尚的武功。

然而，虽然少林寺很大。少林的和尚也很多，不过少林的武功肯定更多，毕竟研究机构是7*24小时工

作的，随时都有新的武功被研究出来，这些被研究出来的武功如果没有人学习，很快就会消失，变成

记录在藏经阁里的一本小册子。过不了多久，就消失在浩瀚的藏经阁中了。

怎么办?

这就是少林俗家弟子的作用了。

少林寺招收俗家弟子是没有限制的：既没有人数限制，也没有资质限制，也没有授徒限制。几乎少林

寺的所有人都可以收俗家弟子。当然，也要有人愿意跟你学。

这样一来，在少林寺的外围就有了一个庞大的群体，这个群里人数众多，资质参差不齐。这些弟子可

以学一些新研究出来的武功，比如判官笔，比如伏虎拳的新招式等等。

这样一来，除了那些真正优秀的武功在少林寺内被反复锤炼，最后形成新的绝技外，大量研究出来的

优秀武功也被遍布江湖的武林人士学习、传播、传承。

许多年后，江湖传说着：“天下武功出少林”。

而少林七十二绝技也想当然地“冠绝天下”。

26 AUGUST

Baker Company: Research Strategy

Baker’s aim is to come up with a small number of drugs in each field that are clearly superior and

offer significant advances to medical practice.

The strategy of Baker Company is completely different.

Its research lab, perhaps the most famous in the pharmaceutical industry, works in an enormous

number of fields.

It does not, however, enter a field until the basic scientific theoretical work has been done.

Then it goes to work.

Of every ten products that come out of its own laboratory, the company itself markets no more

than two or three.

When it becomes reasonably clear that an effective drug will result from a line of research, the

company carefully scrutinizes the product and, indeed, the entire field.

First, is the new product likely to be medically so superior as to become the new “standard”?

Second, is it likely to have major impact throughout the field of health care and medical practice

rather than be confined to one specialty area, even a large one?

And finally, is it likely to remain the “standard” for a good many years, rather than to be overtaken

by competitive products?

If the answer to any of these three questions is “No,” the company will license or sell the

development, rather than convert it into a product of its own.

This has been highly profitable in two ways. It has generated licensing income almost equal to the

profits the company makes on the drugs it makes and sells under its own name.

And it has assured that each of the company’s products is considered the “leader” by the medical

profession.

ACTION POINT: Test your organization’s innovative strategy against that of Baker Company.
