0001 a-bloom

Pronunciation

/əˈbluːm/ (uh-BLOOM)

Etymology & Root

  • a- (on) + bloom (from Old English blōwan “flower”).

English-to-English explanation

In a state of blooming; covered with flowers (archaic/poetic).

Example:

He was standing before a garden, all a-bloom with roses.

English-to-English explanation
