’as to ’是什么意思呢？看起来像介词的样子？

文/韩宝龙

hbaolong@vip.qq.com

确实，’as to’是介词，而且是不常用的介词，这就德鲁克这样的old fashion 的老东西才会用的，意思是：关于，就……而言；根据，按照。

那么，什么是介词呢？介词是英语的“关系胶水”，它把名词/代词和句子其他部分“粘”在一起，告诉我们： 谁、在哪儿、何时、为什么、怎么做的怎么样。 没有介词，英语句子就散架了。

我们来整理一下介词吧

或者，换个分类方法，更容易理解：

按“时间、地点、原因、方式”四大类重新整理（2025最实用版）

1. 时间类（When / How long / How often）

介词短语 意思 例句
at 9 o’clock / at night 具体时刻 We meet at 7 p.m.
on Monday / on 15 May 具体日子/日期 The exam is on Friday.
in the morning / in 2025 时段、月、年 I’ll call you in the afternoon.
for 3 hours / for ages 持续多久 I waited for two hours.
since 2019 从某时开始一直到现在 She’s lived here since 2020.
during the meeting 在……期间 No phones during class.
until / till Friday 一直到 I’m on holiday until next Monday.
before / after lunch 在……之前/之后 Finish it before midnight.
by Friday 不晚于 Submit by 5 p.m. Friday.

2. 地点/位置类（Where）

介词短语 意思 例句
in London / in the room 在一个区域/空间内 She’s in the kitchen.
on the table / on TV 在表面上 Your keys are on the desk.
at the station / at work 具体点、功能性地点 I’ll meet you at the cinema.
under / below the bed 在……下面 The cat is under the table.
over / above the clouds 在……上方 The plane flew over the mountains.
behind / in front of 后面 / 前面 He parked behind the building.
next to / beside 紧挨着 Sit next to me.
between / among 在两者之间 / 在多者之间 Between you and me… / among the trees
across from / opposite 正对面 The bank is opposite the post office.
near / close to 靠近 We live near the station.

3. 原因/目的类（Why / For what）

介词短语 意思 例句
because of 因为 Cancelled because of rain.
due to 由于 Delayed due to technical issues.
thanks to 多亏（可正面可反讽） Thanks to you, we won!
for 为了（目的） I bought it for my mum.
out of 出于（感情、材料） Out of curiosity / out of wood
from 由于（某种来源） He died from cancer.
with 带着（某种情绪） She was shaking with anger.

4. 方式/工具/伴随类（How / With what / With whom）

介词短语 意思 例句
with a knife / with cash 用某种工具 Cut it with a knife.
by email / by car 通过某种方式 I’ll send it by email.
on foot / on the phone 靠…… / 在……上 We came on foot.
in pencil / in English 用某种语言、材料 Write it in pen, not pencil.
like 像……一样 He runs like a cheetah.
as 作为 She works as a teacher.
with my friends 和……一起 I went with Sarah.

终极记忆口诀（背完这四句就够用了）

  • 时间：at点 on日 in月年，for多久 since起点看
  • 地点：in区域 on表面 at具体点，over above under below 分高低
  • 原因：because of = due to，thanks to 看语境
  • 方式：by交通 with工具，in语言 like比喻
