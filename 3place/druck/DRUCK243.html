透过金庸读懂德鲁克

管理必须保持变化和连续性之间的动态平衡。

管理是平衡的艺术，是综合考虑现在和未来后作出决定的艺术。

管理者必须理解和关注现在，不然就没有失去生存的基础。

管理者又必须分析和着眼未来，不然他们就会走着走着就没路了，或者坠入悬崖，或者遇见一堵无边

无际的墙。

每天醒来，管理者就既要考虑现在，考虑今天的工资、房租、业务；也要考虑明天，考虑团队、方

向、市场。

哪个重要？哪个次要？其实都重要，管理者可以分时去考虑：早上考虑现在，下午考虑未来，晚上有

时间的话，可以想想自己。（假如还记得自己的话）。

企业就是一个永不停止的独轮车，推车的这个人，既要推着车前行，因为慢了、停下来，车就会倒

了，翻了。

那么，是不是一直推就好了？当然不是，你还需要快速地判断方向，判断速度是否合理。

哎，干嘛创业呀？闲的！

31 AUGUST

Maintaining Dynamic Equilibrium

Management has to maintain the dynamic equilibrium between change and continuity.

My publisher in Japan, Diamond, recently published selected essays of mine, written over the last

fifty years, under the title The Future Which Already Happened (The Ecological Vision, 1993).

For this book, I wrote a kind of intellectual autobiography, which constitutes the last chapter of the

book.

In it, I record the beginning of my work more than sixty years ago, which was concerned with the

balance between change and continuity.

It was this concern that, ten years later, led me to the study of management.

For I see in management, the specific organ of society that has to maintain the dynamic equilibrium

between change and continuity, without which societies, organizations, and individuals perish.

ACTION POINT:

Institute a systematic process of innovation to lead change.

Drucker on Asia
