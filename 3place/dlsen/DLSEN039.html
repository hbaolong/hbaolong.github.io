BIBLIOGRAPHY

This bibliography includes three main categories of references, under two headings—General Reference and Other Works. First, it lists a number of valuable texts that are relevant to several parts of this book, which may or may not have been cited. Second, it gives full references for all works appearing in the notes; some of these sources are general synoptic studies, and some are more specialized. Third, it gives full references for all the works cited under “Further Reading” at the end of each chapter. These consist almost entirely of works that should be accessible to the general reader (though not necessarily with ease); most are synoptic or introductory in nature.
