0001 blow

Pronunciation

/bloʊ/ (BLOH)

Etymology & Root

  • Old English blāwan “blow air”.

  • Root: Germanic blē- “blow”.

English-to-English explanation

To move air; a hard hit; to flower (for plants).

Example:

The verdict, delivered in Malaysia’s administrative capital Putrajaya, is the second blow in the same week to the embattled former leader, who has been imprisoned since 2022.

0004 corruption

Pronunciation

/kəˈrʌp.ʃən/ (kə-RUP-shən)

Etymology & Root

  • Latin corruptio “rotting” ← corrumpere “spoil”.

English-to-English explanation

Dishonest or illegal behaviour, especially by people in power; the process of becoming morally bad.

Example:
