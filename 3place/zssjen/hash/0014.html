0001 breakthrough

Pronunciation

/ˈbreɪk.θruː/ (BRAYK-throo)

Etymology & Root

  • break + through (20th century military term).

English-to-English explanation

A sudden important development or achievement that removes obstacles.

Example:

The breakthrough came after days of talks between the two countries, with diplomatic encouragement from China and the US.
