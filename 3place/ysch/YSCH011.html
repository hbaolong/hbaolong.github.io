3频词汇检测

Choose the best answer for each blank.

  1. The programme begins with a short film devised by the antis—those opposed to women having the vote—depicting a suffragette as a fierce harridan bullying her poor, ______ husband. (IELTS3,T4,R2)

A. abused B. accurate C. acquired D. admitted

  1. If you don ’ t want to cook indoors, you can borrow a ______ if you ask in advance for one to be made available, and there ’ s even a picnic blanket to sit on outside your house. (IELTS7,T1, L2)

A. ban B. bibliography C. blast D. barbecue

  1. Beyond the practical need to make order out of ______, the rise of dictionaries is associated with the rise of the English middle class, who were anxious to define and circumscribe the various worlds to conquer—lexical as well as social and commercial. (IELTS5,T1,R1)

A. catalogue B. chaos C. chase D. compromise

  1. Eastern Energy uses various types of meter ranging from the traditional dial meter to new technology ______ display meters. (IELTS7,TA,R1)

A. digital B. dynamic C. desirable D. deliberate

  1. We can, perhaps, attribute mediocre results to an ______ placebo effect. (IELTS7,T1,R3)

A. incorporate B. immense C. inadequate D. immune

  1. So ordinary and routine has this become to us that it takes a determined ______ of the imagination to grasp the impact of those first moving images. (IELTS6,T3,R1)

A. leap B. layout C. legislation D. liquid

  1. The effects of ______ literacy programmes can he seen very quickly. (IELTS6,T4,R2)

A. marginal B. maternal C. master D. migrant

  1. Fish and dolphins that live in extremely muddy water cannot see because, although there is light, it is ______ and scattered by the dirt in the water. (IELTS7,T1,R1)

A. operational B. opposed C. obstructed D. overviewed

  1. In a ______ talk, the teacher introduces them to the material to be covered, but does not ‘teach’ it. (IELTS7,T1,R3)

A. premier B. provisional C. pointless D. preliminary

ANSWERS答案

  1. A 2. D 3. B 4. A 5. C

  2. A 7. B 8. C 9. D
